# Roadmap Principles

- Roadmap follows architecture before implementation.
- Completed phases are never redesigned without a verified bug.
- New features must support the long-term product vision.
- Every phase must leave the architecture more modular, scalable, and reusable.
- AlphaEdge AI evolves incrementally through production-ready sprints.

---

# Roadmap Motto

> Every completed phase should make AlphaEdge AI closer to becoming the world's most trusted AI-assisted Trading Intelligence Platform.
