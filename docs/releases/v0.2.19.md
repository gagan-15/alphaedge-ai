# Overview

This release introduces the Market Scanner Engine, extending
AlphaEdge AI's orchestration layer to coordinate the scanning of
multiple stock symbols while integrating with the existing
screening pipeline.

The Market Scanner Engine provides configurable scan limits and
produces standardized market scanning results, preparing the
platform for future large-scale market scanning capabilities.
